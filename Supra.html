Supra

The Toyota Supra is an iconic sports car known for its powerful performance, sleek design, and impressive speed.

Specifications

  • Engine: 3.0L Twin-Turbo Inline-6
  • Horsepower: 382 HP
  • Torque: 368 lb-ft
  • Top Speed: 155 mph
  • Transmission: 8-speed Automatic / 6-speed Manual

Price

The starting price of the Toyota Supra is around $45,000, varying by model and region.
