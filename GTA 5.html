GTA 5 - Overview

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA 5) is an open-world action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North. Set in the fictional state of San Andreas, it follows three protagonists: Michael, Trevor, and Franklin, as they navigate the criminal underworld.

Gameplay Features

  • Open-world environment with vast cities and countryside.
  • Play as three different protagonists with interwoven stories.
  • Huge variety of vehicles including cars, planes, and boats.
  • Realistic AI and dynamic world interactions.
  • Online multiplayer mode - GTA Online.

System Requirements

  • Minimum: Intel Core 2 Quad CPU Q6600 / 4GB RAM / NVIDIA 9800 GT / 65GB Storage
  • Recommended: Intel i5 3470 / 8GB RAM / NVIDIA GTX 660 / 65GB Storage

Price & Availability

GTA 5 is available on PC, PlayStation, and Xbox platforms. Prices vary by region and discounts may apply during sales.
