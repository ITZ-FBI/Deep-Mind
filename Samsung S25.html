Samsung S25

The Samsung S25 is the latest flagship smartphone from Samsung, featuring cutting-edge technology, a stunning display, and powerful performance.

Specifications

  • Display: 6.7-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X
  • Processor: Exynos 2400 / Snapdragon 8 Gen 3
  • Camera: 50MP (Main) + 12MP (Ultra-wide) + 10MP (Telephoto)
  • Battery: 4500mAh with 45W fast charging
  • Operating System: Android 14 with One UI

Price

The starting price of the Samsung S25 is around $999, varying by region and storage options.
