BMW M3

The BMW M3 is a high-performance version of the BMW 3 Series, developed by BMW's in-house motorsport division, BMW M. Known for its power, agility, and luxury, the M3 is a favorite among car enthusiasts.

Specifications

  • Engine: 3.0L Twin-Turbo Inline-6
  • Horsepower: Up to 503 HP
  • 0-60 mph: Around 3.8 seconds
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual / 8-speed automatic
  • Drive Type: RWD / AWD
  • Top Speed: 180 mph (electronically limited)

Price

The starting price of the BMW M3 is around $74,000. Higher trims and optional packages can increase the price to over $100,000.

Origin

The BMW M3 was first introduced in 1986 as a high-performance variant of the BMW 3 Series. The latest model, the G80, continues the legacy with cutting-edge technology and enhanced performance capabilities.

How to Use

The BMW M3 is designed for both daily driving and high-performance track use. It comes with multiple driving modes, including Comfort, Sport, and Track, allowing the driver to customize performance based on their needs.

Maintenance

Regular maintenance is crucial to keep the BMW M3 in peak condition. Key aspects include:

  • Oil changes every 7,500 miles
  • Brake inspections every 10,000 miles
  • Tire rotations every 5,000 miles
  • Cooling system and transmission fluid checks
  • Battery and electrical system checks

Why Choose the BMW M3?

The BMW M3 is a blend of luxury and high performance, making it ideal for enthusiasts who want a sports sedan that excels on both the road and the track.
