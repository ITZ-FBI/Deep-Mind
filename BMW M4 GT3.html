BMW M4 GT3 

The BMW M4 is a high-performance version of the BMW 4 Series automobile developed by BMW's motorsport division, BMW M, that has been built since 2014. As part of the renumbering that splits the coupé and convertible variants of the 3 Series into the 4 Series, the M4 replaced those variants of the BMW M3. Upgrades over the standard BMW 4 Series include an upgraded engine, suspension, exhaust system, brakes and weight reduction measures including increased use of carbon fiber, such as on the roof of the car.

Overview

The BMW M4 GT3 is a high-performance race car designed for professional GT racing. It replaces the M6 GT3 and offers enhanced aerodynamics, improved drivability, and reduced running costs while maintaining BMW’s racing heritage.

Specifications

  • Engine: 3.0L Twin-Turbo Inline-6 (S58 engine, race-tuned)
  • Power Output: Up to 590 hp (depending on balance of performance regulations)
  • Torque: Over 700 Nm
  • Transmission: 6-speed sequential racing gearbox
  • Chassis: Lightweight carbon-fiber-reinforced plastic (CFRP) body
  • Aerodynamics: Advanced adjustable wing and diffuser for maximum downforce
  • Weight: Approx. 1,250 kg (varies based on regulations)
  • Suspension: Adjustable double wishbone suspension with KW dampers
  • Brakes: High-performance motorsport braking system with ventilated discs
  • Fuel Tank: FIA-approved endurance racing fuel cell (120L capacity)
  • Wheels: 18-inch center-lock racing wheels

Performance

  • Top Speed: Over 280 km/h (174 mph)
  • 0-100 km/h (0-62 mph): Approx. 3.2 seconds
  • Downforce: Optimized aerodynamic package for superior cornering grip
  • Traction Control: Advanced Bosch Motorsport ABS & traction control system

Technology & Features

  • Driver Display: Fully digital 10-inch racing display
  • Steering Wheel: Multifunction racing wheel with programmable buttons
  • Telemetry: Advanced data logging and real-time vehicle monitoring
  • Cooling System: Optimized water and oil cooling for endurance racing

Safety

  • Roll Cage: FIA-homologated welded steel roll cage
  • Fire Suppression: Integrated fire suppression system
  • Seat: FIA-certified racing bucket seat with 6-point harness
  • Fuel Safety: FIA-approved explosion-proof fuel tank

Pricing & Availability

The BMW M4 GT3 is priced at approximately $530,000. Additional costs may apply for spares, custom configurations, and team support packages. Available for purchase through BMW Motorsport.

Maintenance & Running Costs

  • Engine Service Life: 10,000 km (approx.) before rebuild
  • Gearbox Service Life: 5,000 km (approx.) before rebuild
  • Brake Pad Replacement: Every 2-3 races
  • Annual Running Costs: Estimated at $100,000 - $250,000 per season

Competitors

  • Audi R8 LMS GT3
  • Mercedes-AMG GT3
  • Porsche 911 GT3 R
  • Ferrari 296 GT3
  • Lamborghini Huracán GT3 EVO2

Customer Support

BMW Motorsport provides full customer racing support, including spare parts supply, technical assistance, and software updates for teams and private owners.
